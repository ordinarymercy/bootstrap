About Me

Susan Lutz, who lives in New Orleans, has encountered a few bumps in the road due to health matters, but is now ready to roll up her sleeves and get back to work!

Susan recently completed her Web Design Professional Certificate Course at LSUE via Ed2Go, with an "A" average, and will continue her studies with SEO and UI/UX training. Susan was drawn to web design in part due to the monthly newsletter that she produces on a volunteer basis for her community. She has been producing that newsletter since 2017. She enjoys collaborating on the newsletter as well as formatting and putting together the articles, and came to the realization that web design is much like her work on the newsletter, except that it involves coding.

As a veteran of the United States Air Force, Susan was trained to have exceptional attention to detail and follow-through. Susan was awarded Veteran of the Month in January 2024 from the New Orleans VA Hospital, where she worked. Through her schooling, she has learned Adobe Photoshop, Adobe Animate, HTML, CSS, some JavaScript and Bootstrap. Susan is eager to put her skills to the test. Susan stays busy with volunteering, socializing, and exercising.
