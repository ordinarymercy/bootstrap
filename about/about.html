About Me

I am Susan Lutz, and I live in New Orleans. I've encountered a few bumps in the road due to health matters, but I'm now ready to roll up my sleeves and get back to work!

I recently completed my Web Design Professional Certificate Course at LSUE via Ed2Go, with an "A" average, and will continue my studies with SEO and UI/UX training. I was drawn to web design in part due to the monthly newsletter that I produce on a volunteer basis for my community. I have been producing that newsletter since 2017. I enjoy collaborating on the newsletter as well as formatting and putting together the articles, and came to the realization that web design is much like my work on the newsletter, except that it involves coding.

As a veteran of the United States Air Force, I was trained to have exceptional attention to detail and follow-through. I was awarded Veteran of the Month in January 2024 from the New Orleans VA Hospital, where I worked. Through my schooling, I have learned Adobe Photoshop, Adobe Animate, HTML, CSS, some JavaScript and Bootstrap. I am eager to put my skills to the test.
