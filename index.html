Welcome to My Site

I am enrolled in the Web Design Professional Certificate course at Louisiana State University Eunice, via Ed2Go, and will complete the program with an "A" before July of 2025. I'm excited about my future as a web designer!

You should consider hiring me because I excel at asking the right questions to gather the essential information needed to create a website that meets or exceeds your expectations. Additionally, I possess strong organizational skills, utilizing standard naming conventions and folder structures for each client. I have developed this website mostly from the ground up using HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and Bootstrap, without relying on any Content Management Systems like Wix or WordPress. The exception is that I used a Google Slideshow for my portfolio, as I found that the bootstrap options were limited. But, if I can accomplish this through manual coding, just imagine what I could achieve with a CMS!
